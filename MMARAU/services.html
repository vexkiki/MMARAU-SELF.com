Our Services

Comprehensive Student Services at Maasai Mara University

At Maasai Mara University, we are committed to offering an array of services that empower students to succeed academically, socially, and professionally. These services are designed to provide the necessary support and resources for a fulfilling university experience.

Academic Services:

  • Academic Advising: Personalized guidance to help students navigate their academic journey and select courses that align with their career goals.
  • Tutoring Services: Assistance for students who may need help in specific subjects or assignments to ensure academic success.
  • Research Support: A dedicated team offering support for students undertaking research projects, including access to resources and academic mentorship.

Career Services:

  • Career Counseling: One-on-one sessions with career counselors to help students plan their careers, prepare resumes, and develop interview skills.
  • Job Placement Services: Partnerships with local and international organizations to offer students internship and job placement opportunities.
  • Alumni Networking: A robust network of alumni to help students connect with potential mentors and employers.

Student Life & Wellness:

  • Sports and Recreation: State-of-the-art facilities for students to engage in various sports activities, promoting physical well-being and teamwork.
  • Counseling and Mental Health Support: Professional counseling services to help students manage stress, anxiety, and personal challenges.
  • Student Clubs and Societies: A wide range of clubs and societies for students to explore their interests and hobbies while building lasting friendships.
