Our Departments

Explore Our Academic Departments

Maasai Mara University is home to a variety of academic departments that cater to a wide range of interests and career goals. Whether you want to build a career in technology, business, or the arts, we have the programs to suit your aspirations.

Departments Offered

  • Department of Computer Science: A place for future tech leaders, focused on programming, software development, and IT infrastructure.
  • Department of Business Administration: Shaping the next generation of business leaders and entrepreneurs with practical knowledge.
  • Department of Education: Empowering individuals to become inspiring teachers, counselors, and education administrators.
  • Department of Engineering: Developing problem-solvers skilled in designing and implementing innovative solutions.
  • Department of Social Sciences: Exploring the complexities of human behavior and society to create impactful change.
