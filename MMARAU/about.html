About Us

Our Mission

At Maasai Mara University, we are dedicated to offering an inclusive, accessible, and affordable education. Our mission is to cultivate the intellectual, personal, and social growth of students while contributing to the overall development of the community.

Our Core Values:

  • Excellence: We continuously strive for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct in all our interactions.
  • Collaboration: We foster a collaborative and supportive environment among faculty, staff, and students.
  • Innovation: We encourage creative solutions and the exploration of new ideas.

A Glimpse into Our History:

Founded in 2008, Maasai Mara University has made remarkable strides in advancing education and research in Kenya. With a focus on sustainability and community involvement, we continue to empower students to become leaders in their fields.
